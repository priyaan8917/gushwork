Frequently Asked Questions

What are the advantages of HDPE pipes over traditional pipes? ▲
HDPE pipes offer superior chemical resistance, flexibility, leak-proof joints, longer service life (50+ years), lower maintenance costs, and are environmentally friendly. They don't corrode, rust, or require coating unlike metal pipes.
What is the life expectancy of HDPE pipes? ▼
HDPE pipes have a service life of 50-100 years under normal operating conditions, making them one of the most durable piping materials available.
Can HDPE pipes handle hot water applications? ▼
Standard HDPE pipes can handle temperatures up to 80°C. For higher temperature applications, we offer PE-RT pipes specifically designed for hot water systems.
What sizes are available? ▼
We manufacture HDPE pipes from 20mm to 1600mm diameter in various pressure ratings (PN 2.5 to PN 16) to meet diverse application requirements.
Are your pipes BIS certified? ▼
Yes, all our HDPE pipes are manufactured according to IS 4984 standards and carry BIS certification along with ISO 9001:2015 quality certification.
